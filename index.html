Tuition Fees Waiver Scheme
Under this scheme an additional 5% students will be admitted as per the guidelines of the CGPET 2014 Admission Policy framed by the State Government. This intake will be over and above the approved intake. However, TFW will be applicable only if the number of students admitted is exceeding the approved intake.

Reservation Policy In Admission
- The Institute strictly adheres to the reservation policy framed by the State Government vide gazette notification bearing No. F9-02/2012/T.E./42 dated 19.02.2013, which stipulates as under:
‚ÄúFrom academic session 2013-14 and onwards, 12%, 32% and 14% of total sanctioned seats in different courses provided by private unaided technical educational institutions shall be reserved for the candidates of Scheduled Castes (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST) and Other Backward Classes (OBC) respectively for the purpose of admission in the State.

Scholarship
GATE scholarship is provided to the students who have qualified GATE Scorecard and as per the rules framed by AICTE, New Delhi.
